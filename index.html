About Me

With over 20 years of experience in the lighting industry and audiovisual systems integration, I have had the privilege of collaborating with world-renowned artists such as Coldplay, Harry Styles,Mumford and sons and many mores, to create unforgettable live experiences. My expertise spans across project management, team leadership, and extensive experience as a touring technician. I excel in mastering documentation processes and utilizing a wide range of software tools, including Vectorworks, CAD, and more. , ensuring that every show is a visual masterpiece.

I also specialize in lighting consulting for new multidisciplinary spaces, working closely with architects, engineers, and project managers to design cutting-edge lighting and audiovisual systems. My goal is to enhance the functionality and aesthetic of each space, creating environments that inspire and engage.
